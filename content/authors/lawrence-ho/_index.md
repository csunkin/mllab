Lawrence Ho earned his Bachelor of Science in Kinesiology and Exercise Science from California State University, Northridge. After graduation, he relocated to Singapore, where he worked in athlete life management at the National Youth Sports Institute (2016-2017) and Gallup (2018-2019). He now serves as Team Lead, APAC, at Statista, leading sales and business development in the Asia-Pacific region since 2023.
