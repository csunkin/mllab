Blake Bingham is an undergraduate student at CSUN, majoring in Kinesiology with an emphasis in Exercise Science. Blake started at Los Angeles Pierce College graduating with an Associate’s degree in Kinesiology. He transferred to CSUN in the Fall of 2022 to continue his human movement journey. This is his third semester with the Move-Learn Lab and has taken the role of lab manager. After obtaining his Bachelor’s degree, Blake aspires to continue his education in a Doctorate of Physical Therapy program. He hopes to become a physical therapist and work in an inpatient care setting. Outside of the lab, Blake writes and records music, enjoys working out, and playing in sports leagues.
