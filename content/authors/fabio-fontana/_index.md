Fabio Fontana, PhD, is the Department Head and Professor of Kinesiology and Physical Education at the University of Northern Iowa. He has been with UNI since 2008 and serves as the graduate coordinator for the Physical Education graduate program. 

His research explores the intersection of vision in sports and weight discrimination, with numerous joint publications alongside Dr. Furtado.
