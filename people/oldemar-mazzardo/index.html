Oldemar Mazzardo Júnior

Associate Professor

Unioeste (Universidade Estadual do Oeste do Paraná)

Oldemar Mazzardo Júnior is an associate professor of Physical Education at Unioeste (Universidade Estadual do Oeste do Paraná), Brazil. His expertise centers on motor development, children's movement skills, physical activity, and interdisciplinary education practices.

With collaborative research alongside Ovande Furtado Jr.—including 2018 and 2024 studies on motor skills and fitness in youth—he contributes to our lab's focus on applied kinesiology and psychomotor interventions. Mazzardo coordinates Unioeste's undergraduate program and supervises theses on psychomotor learning.
