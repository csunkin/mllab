Brittney Golnick

Research Assistant

Topanga Montessori Preschool

Brittney Golnick is a kinesiology-trained educator based in Greater Los Angeles. A CSUN graduate with experience presenting in KIN 479, she currently serves as a teaching assistant at Topanga Montessori Preschool while bringing her high school athletics background from El Camino Real Royals JV basketball to our research lab. In this role, she focuses on neuromuscular responses and pediatric motor development.
