Development and psychometric evaluation of the PictoMotion assessment tool

Furtado

PictoMotion uses pictorial representations to facilitate the observation and evaluation of children's fundamental motor skills.

Project name:Development and psychometric evaluation of the PictoMotion assessment tool
Responsible:Ovande Furtado Jr
Status:Ongoing
Related links:None available

This project aims to collect validity and reliability evidence for the PictoMotion, an instrument designed to assess the qualitative aspects of fundamental motor skills in children. PictoMotion uses pictorial representations to facilitate the observation and evaluation of children's movement patterns during various motor tasks.
